Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: RunTroop is designed for running club safety. We only collect data necessary to keep you and your running group safe, and we never sell your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Display name - Shown to other members in your running clubs
  • Password - Encrypted and securely stored (we never see your actual password)

Location Data

  • GPS coordinates - Collected in real-time only during active runs
  • Accuracy information - To ensure reliable tracking
  • Speed and heading - To show your movement on the map
  • Altitude - For elevation tracking

Club Information

  • Club memberships - Which clubs you've joined or created
  • Club roles - Whether you're an owner or member
  • Run participation - Records of runs you've joined

Route Data

  • GPX files - Uploaded by club owners for planned routes
  • Route metadata - Distance, elevation, and coordinates

2. How We Use Your Information

Safety and Core Functionality

  • Display your real-time location to other participants during active runs
  • Enable club owners to monitor group safety
  • Show your position on the shared map
  • Calculate distances and routes traveled

Service Improvements

  • Improve location accuracy algorithms
  • Optimize battery usage during tracking
  • Fix bugs and enhance app performance

Communication

  • Send important service notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you of policy changes

3. When Location Data is Collected

Important: Location tracking only happens when you actively join a run. We do not track your location at any other time.

Active Run Tracking:

  • Starts when you tap "Join Run" or "Start Run"
  • Updates every 5 seconds or when you move 20 meters
  • Stops when the run ends or you leave the run
  • Requires your explicit permission via device location settings

Location is NOT collected:

  • When browsing clubs or routes
  • When the app is closed
  • When you're not in an active run
  • After a run has ended

4. Who Can See Your Location

During Active Runs:

  • All participants in the same active run can see each other's real-time locations
  • Your display name is shown with your location marker
  • Your route path is visible to show where you've been during the run

After Runs End:

  • Historical location data is retained for 30 days for safety records
  • After 30 days, location data is automatically deleted
  • Only statistical data (distance traveled, run duration) is kept long-term

Never Shared:

  • Your location is never visible outside your running clubs
  • We never sell location data to third parties
  • We don't use location data for advertising

5. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

All data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider with enterprise-grade security:

  • Data centers located in secure facilities
  • Industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Regular security audits and updates

Access Controls

  • Row Level Security (RLS) - Database-level protection ensuring users only see their own data
  • Authentication - Secure login required for all features
  • Club-based permissions - You can only see data for clubs you're a member of

6. Your Rights and Controls

You Can:

  • Leave a run - Stop location sharing instantly by leaving the active run
  • Leave a club - Remove yourself from any club at any time
  • Delete your account - Permanently remove all your data (see Data Deletion Policy)
  • Revoke location permissions - Turn off location access in your device settings
  • Request your data - Get a copy of all data we have about you

Data Retention

  • Location data - Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Account data - Retained until you delete your account
  • Run statistics - Anonymized and kept for historical records

7. Third-Party Services

RunTroop uses the following trusted services:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

  • Stores all user data, location records, and club information
  • Handles secure authentication
  • Privacy Policy: supabase.com/privacy

Google Maps (iOS & Android)

  • Displays maps and location markers
  • Does not receive identifying information
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Expo (App Infrastructure)

  • Provides app update delivery
  • Crash reporting (anonymous)
  • Privacy Policy: expo.dev/privacy

8. Children's Privacy

RunTroop is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Users

If you're using RunTroop outside the United Kingdom, please note:

  • Data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws
  • We comply with GDPR for EU users
  • We maintain appropriate safeguards regardless of location

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will change
  • We'll notify you in the app of significant changes
  • Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@runtroop.app
  • Support: support@runtroop.app
  • Data Deletion: See our Data Deletion Policy
