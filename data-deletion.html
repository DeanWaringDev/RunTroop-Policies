Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Your Data, Your Choice: You have complete control over your data. This policy explains how to delete your information from RunTroop.

1. Automatic Data Deletion

Location Data

RunTroop automatically deletes certain data after a specified period:

  • GPS location points - Deleted automatically after 30 days
  • Run tracking data - Historical positions removed after 30 days
  • Real-time location - Cleared when you leave a run or the run ends

What Remains After 30 Days

Only anonymized, statistical data is retained:

  • Total distance traveled (no location coordinates)
  • Run duration and date
  • Number of participants (no personal identifiers)

2. Manual Account Deletion

Complete Removal: Deleting your account permanently removes all your personal data from RunTroop.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Coming Soon)

  1. Open the RunTroop app
  2. Go to Settings/Profile
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
  5. Your account and data will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to delete@runtroop.app with:

Subject: Delete My RunTroop Account

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your display name (if different)
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete all data

Response Time

  • We'll confirm receipt within 24 hours
  • Account deletion will be completed within 7 days
  • You'll receive confirmation when deletion is complete

3. What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

Personal Information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Password (encrypted hash)
  • Profile data
  • Authentication tokens

Activity Data

  • All GPS location history
  • Run participation records
  • Club memberships
  • Routes you've created or uploaded

Usage Data

  • Device information
  • App usage statistics
  • Login history

4. What May Be Retained

Important: Certain data may be retained for legal or operational reasons.

Anonymized Statistics

We may retain aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you:

  • Total number of users (count only)
  • Average distances run (no individual data)
  • App usage trends (aggregated)

Legal Requirements

Data may be retained if required by law:

  • Court orders or legal proceedings
  • Compliance with regulations
  • Investigation of terms violations
  • Financial records (if applicable)

Backup Systems

  • Data in backup systems will be deleted within 90 days
  • Backups are for disaster recovery only
  • We cannot restore your account after deletion

5. Impact on Running Clubs

If You're a Club Member

  • You'll be removed from all clubs automatically
  • Your past run participation records will be anonymized
  • Other members won't see your personal information

If You're a Club Owner

Important for Club Owners: Deleting your account affects clubs you created.

  • You must transfer ownership first if you want the club to continue
  • If you delete without transferring, your clubs will be marked as archived
  • Archived clubs cannot start new runs
  • Members can still leave archived clubs
  • After 30 days, archived clubs are permanently deleted

Transferring Club Ownership Before Deletion

  1. Open your club in the app
  2. Go to Club Settings
  3. Select "Transfer Ownership"
  4. Choose a trusted member
  5. Confirm the transfer
  6. Then proceed with account deletion

6. Data Deletion from Third-Party Services

RunTroop uses external services that also store data:

Supabase (Database)

  • Data is deleted from our database immediately
  • Backups are purged within 90 days
  • Supabase complies with GDPR deletion requirements

Google Maps

  • Google Maps only receives anonymized location data
  • No personal data is sent to Google Maps
  • Map tiles are cached temporarily on your device only

Expo Services

  • Expo only stores app crash logs (anonymous)
  • No personal data is sent to Expo
  • Crash logs are automatically deleted after 90 days

7. Alternatives to Full Deletion

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, consider these options:

Leave Clubs Instead

  • You can leave individual clubs without deleting your account
  • Your location won't be shared with that club anymore
  • You can rejoin later if you change your mind

Revoke Location Permissions

  • Turn off location access in your device settings
  • The app won't track you without permission
  • You can still browse clubs and routes

Temporary Break

  • Simply stop using the app
  • Your account remains but inactive
  • Location data auto-deletes after 30 days anyway
  • No data is collected when you're not using the app

8. Reactivation After Deletion

Deletion is Permanent: Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

  • You cannot reactivate a deleted account
  • You can create a new account with the same email
  • Your previous clubs and data will not be restored
  • You'll need to rejoin clubs with new invitation codes

9. Children's Data

For users under 18:

  • Parents/guardians can request deletion on your behalf
  • Email delete@runtroop.app with proof of guardianship
  • We'll verify the relationship before proceeding
  • Deletion follows the same process as adult accounts

10. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have additional rights:

Right to be Forgotten

  • You can request complete data erasure
  • We must comply within 30 days
  • Exceptions apply for legal obligations

Right to Data Portability

  • Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Data provided in machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Email data@runtroop.app for data export

Right to Restrict Processing

  • Request temporary suspension of data processing
  • Your data remains but is not actively used
  • Useful if you're disputing data accuracy

11. Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we verify all deletion requests:

What We'll Ask For

  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation from that email account
  • Answer to a security question (if applicable)

Why We Verify

  • Prevents unauthorized account deletion
  • Protects you from malicious requests
  • Ensures the real account owner is making the request

12. Deletion Timeline

Timeframe What Happens
Within 24 hours Request acknowledged via email
Within 7 days Account and data deleted from active systems
Within 30 days Data removed from live backups
Within 90 days Complete removal from all backup systems
